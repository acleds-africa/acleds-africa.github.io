Get Involved

Collaborate With ACLEDS

We actively seek partnerships with researchers, institutions, schools, government bodies, and development organisations who share our commitment to evidence-based education in Africa.

Ways to Work Together

Partnership Pathways

We engage in several forms of collaboration depending on the nature of the partner and the opportunity.

Research Collaboration

Joint research with academic institutions or independent researchers working in learning analytics, educational data science, mathematics education, or adjacent fields. We are open to co-authorship, data sharing arrangements, and co-supervised graduate projects.

  • Co-investigation on funded projects
  • Joint publication and dissemination
  • Shared dataset development
  • Methodological exchange

School Partnerships

We work directly with schools willing to participate in our longitudinal studies. School partners play a critical role in our data collection and also benefit from access to findings and analytical support that can inform their own practice.

  • Participation in LMLAS and future studies
  • Access to aggregated findings from your school context
  • Teacher professional development opportunities
  • Priority access to research briefs and reports

Government & Policy

We engage with education ministries, curriculum bodies, and district education offices to ensure our research findings reach the policy processes where they can have most impact. We provide evidence summaries, briefings, and consultation.

  • Policy briefs and evidence summaries
  • Curriculum review consultation
  • Education data system design input
  • Capacity building for education officers

Development Partners

International development organisations, foundations, and NGOs working in education across Africa can partner with ACLEDS to embed rigorous research and evaluation into their programmes.

  • Programme evaluation design and delivery
  • Independent research partnerships
  • Data analysis and reporting
  • Research capacity building

Our Commitment

What You Can Expect From Us

We approach every partnership with the same commitment to rigour, transparency, and impact that guides our research.

Clear communication

Regular updates, honest reporting, and accessible language throughout any collaboration.

Ethical practice

Rigorous data protection, participant consent procedures, and transparent research ethics.

Useful outputs

Findings communicated in forms that are useful to partners — not just academic papers.

Long-term orientation

We value sustained relationships over one-off engagements wherever possible.

Graduate Students

Interested in postgraduate supervision?

ACLEDS is open to supervising or co-supervising postgraduate students whose research aligns with our themes. If you are a prospective MPhil or PhD student, please reach out with a brief outline of your research interest.
