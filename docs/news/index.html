Announcement June 2025

ACLEDS Website Launches

We're pleased to launch the ACLEDS website — a home for our research, team, publications, and opportunities to collaborate. The site will grow as our research programme develops.

Publication 1 March 2025

New Working Paper: Mathematics Confidence and Achievement

Grace Amackson's working paper examining the longitudinal relationship between mathematics confidence and achievement is now under review. A preprint will be made available on this site.

Announcement 15 January 2025

ACLEDS Launches the Longitudinal Mathematics Learning Analytics Study

ACLEDS has officially launched its flagship research initiative — a multi-year study tracking how mathematics confidence and achievement develop across primary schooling using learning analytics methods.
