Who We Are

About ACLEDS

A research centre dedicated to generating rigorous, contextually valid knowledge about learning and education in Africa — using the tools of data science.

Our Foundation

Why ACLEDS Exists

Much of the educational research informing policy and practice in African schools originates outside the continent — imported wholesale without sufficient attention to local context, culture, infrastructure, or the specific challenges that characterise African educational systems.

ACLEDS was established to address this gap directly. We produce scholarship that begins with African classrooms, African learners, and African educational realities — and that uses the analytical power of learning analytics and educational data science to surface insights that would otherwise remain hidden.

Our work spans the generation of new knowledge, the development of methodological approaches suited to our context, and the translation of findings into recommendations that can actually reach teachers, curriculum designers, and policymakers.

Our Mission

"To advance evidence-based education in Africa through rigorous, contextually grounded research in learning analytics and educational data science."

Our Approach

  • Longitudinal research designs
  • Mixed methods integration
  • Transparent data practices
  • Policy-focused dissemination
  • School and community partnerships

What Guides Us

Core Commitments

Inclusion

Integrity

Impact

Research designed to reach policy, not just publication.
