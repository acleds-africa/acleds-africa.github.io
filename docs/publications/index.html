Working Paper 2025

Mathematics Confidence and Achievement: A Longitudinal Perspective

Grace Amackson

This paper examines the co-evolution of mathematics confidence and measurable achievement across the primary school years, using longitudinal data from Ghanaian school cohorts. Findings suggest that confidence diverges from achievement in patterned ways that have implications for early intervention.

Research Brief 2025

Applying Learning Analytics in Under-Resourced School Contexts: Opportunities and Constraints

ACLEDS Research Team

A methodological review of the challenges and opportunities facing learning analytics practitioners working in African schools with limited digital infrastructure, inconsistent data recording, and resource-constrained environments.

Conceptual Paper 2024

Educational Data Science for African School Systems: Possibilities and Constraints

ACLEDS

This conceptual paper maps the terrain of educational data science as it might apply to African educational contexts, identifying areas of promise and highlighting the structural constraints — data, infrastructure, capacity — that must be addressed.
