Research Faculty

GA

Grace Amackson

Founding Director & Principal Investigator

Focus
Mathematics education, longitudinal research design, learning analytics

Grace founded ACLEDS with a conviction that African educational systems deserve research generated from within — rigorous, contextually grounded, and oriented toward real policy impact. Her work focuses on how mathematical confidence and identity develop across schooling.
